# 24-Hour Money Plan

## Goal
Get the first paid buyer or serious lead without spending money.

## Hour 0 — Approval
Hans reviews:
- Offer
- Site copy
- Pricing
- Domain/publishing/payment plan

## Hour 1 — Make site publishable
Required decisions:
- Business name: LaunchPage Express or another name
- Contact email
- Payment method: manual invoice, Stripe, PayPal, Wise, etc.
- Hosting/domain choice

## Hours 2-4 — Publish
No-cost options:
- GitHub Pages
- Netlify free tier
- Vercel free tier

Possible paid later:
- Domain: usually ~$10-20/year

## Hours 4-8 — Warm outreach
Post to existing social channels and message warm contacts.
Target: 20-50 personalized messages.

## Hours 8-18 — Cold prospecting
Find prospects with obvious weak/no landing pages.
Target: 50-100 highly relevant prospects.
Offer a free 2-minute page/offer teardown to start conversations.

## Hours 18-24 — Close first buyer
Use scarcity carefully:
- “I have one 24h sprint slot today.”
- “If we start by {{time}}, I can deliver first version by tomorrow.”

## Minimum viable close
If $299 feels too high for first proof, offer:
- $99 paid audit, credited toward sprint
- $149 starter page
- $299 full sprint

## Do not do
- Do not buy ads yet
- Do not overbuild
- Do not promise guaranteed sales
- Do not accept complex app projects as 24h sprint
