# LaunchPage Express — Internal Launch Pack

## Chosen offer
A 24-hour productized landing page sprint for online businesses that need leads, bookings, waitlist signups, or preorders fast.

Why this route:
- Digital delivery only
- Worldwide buyers
- Can be delivered using existing skills/tools
- No inventory, shipping, or ad spend required
- High perceived value vs. low fulfillment cost
- Buyers already understand websites/landing pages

## Suggested pricing to test
- Starter Page: $149, 48h delivery
- 24h Launch Sprint: $299, recommended
- Premium Sprint: $499, includes more launch assets

## Initial target buyers
Prioritize people who already have something to sell but weak/no landing page:
1. Coaches/consultants launching offers
2. Freelancers/productized service providers
3. Small agencies needing quick campaign pages
4. Course/ebook/template creators
5. SaaS founders validating waitlists
6. Local service businesses running a promo

## Fulfillment scope
Included:
- One-page static landing page
- Offer positioning
- Conversion copy
- CTA section
- Mobile responsive HTML/CSS
- Launch checklist and promo copy

Not included in base sprint:
- Complex web apps
- Multi-page sites
- Custom backend
- Paid ads management
- Brand identity system
- Guaranteed sales

## Approval required before external action
Hans must approve before:
- Publishing the site
- Buying a domain
- Creating accounts
- Connecting payment processors
- Sending outreach messages
- Spending any money
