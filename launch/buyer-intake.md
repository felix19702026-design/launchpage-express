# Buyer Intake Questions

Ask buyers these questions before accepting payment:

1. What are you selling?
2. Who is the exact target buyer?
3. What action should the page drive? (book call, buy, preorder, join waitlist, request quote)
4. What price or offer details should be shown?
5. Do you have proof? (testimonials, results, logos, screenshots)
6. Do you have brand assets? (logo, colors, images)
7. What deadline do you need?
8. Where should form submissions go?
9. Do you already have hosting/domain?
10. Any claims we must avoid for legal/compliance reasons?

Do not start external work until scope and payment route are approved by Hans.
